Practical steps for starting a poultry farm include: 1. Conducting market research to identify demand and niches; 2. Writing a business plan covering capital, operating costs, and revenue forecasts; 3. Selecting a business model (contract vs. independent); 4. Registering the business and securing necessary licenses and permits; 5. Choosing a location and setting up infrastructure (sheds, ventilation, feeding systems); 6. Sourcing high-quality chicks and feed; 7. Establishing biosecurity and veterinary management plans; and 8. Building relationships with local distributors or buyers.
