## Opportunities in the Poultry Industry
Opportunities for newcomers exist in underserved or niche segments, such as organic and pasture-raised poultry, specialty heritage breeds, and localized sustainable production systems. Additionally, providing support services like automated equipment installation, digital farm-management consultancy, and specialized feed distribution offers paths to enter the industry without necessarily owning large-scale rearing facilities. There is growing consumer demand for ethically sourced and "farm-to-table" poultry products.
