The global meat and poultry industry is highly competitive and consolidated, featuring major multinational corporations like JBS S.A., Tyson Foods, Cargill, BRF S.A., and Charoen Pokphand Foods. These firms compete on product innovation, pricing, distribution network efficiency, and supply chain strength. Key drivers include shifting consumer dietary habits, demand for protein-rich diets, and the growth of ready-to-eat convenience products, while challenges involve rising animal welfare concerns, environmental regulations, and volatile raw material costs.
