# Poultry Farming Regulations
Poultry farming is heavily regulated to ensure public health, animal welfare, and food safety. Compliance areas include:
* Sanitation standards
* Biosecurity protocols to prevent disease
* Waste management and environmental compliance
* Ethical standards for bird housing

Businesses must often adhere to national or regional agricultural department standards, undergo regular inspections, and hold necessary health and safety certifications for selling eggs or meat.
